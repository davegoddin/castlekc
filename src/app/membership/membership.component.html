Membership

Monthly membership

Membership is £20 per month (£10 for concessions).

Monthly club membership dues cover all training sessions and league match fees for the month. Payments are made via standing order – the club treasurer can provide bank details.

Pay as you go

Training sessions and match fees are £6 per session/fixture (£3 for concessions). Payments can be made via online bank transfer or in cash to the club treasurer – please ensure you have exact change for cash payments.

Concessions

Reduced fees are available for full-time students and jobseekers.

Membership restrictions

Members must be aged 14 or greater.
