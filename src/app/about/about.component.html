Castle Korfball

About us

Founded in 1993, Castle Korfball is the oldest korfball club in the North West of England. We are based in Newcastle-under-Lyme (near Stoke-on-Trent), and currently compete in the West Midlands Korfball League alongside teams from Keele and Birmingham.

New players are always welcome, whether you have played korfball before or are a complete beginner. We are a friendly, mixed-gender, mixed-age group, aiming to keep fit, win matches, and have fun doing it. Come along to a Tuesday training session (free for newcomers) and give it a go for yourself!
