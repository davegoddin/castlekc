Training

What?

Training sessions involve drills to develop your skills, coaching on strategy, and practice games to put it all together. No experience is needed, our coaches are always happy to teach the fundamentals of korfball - you'll be scoring goals in no time!

When?

Weekly on Tuesdays, 8-10pm, all year round.

Where?

Sports hall in the Gordon Banks Sport Centre (Newcastle College).

Address

Gordon Banks Sport Centre

Knutton Lane

Newcastle-under-Lyme

Staffordshire

ST5 2GB

Parking

Parking is free in the car park on the left as you turn into the Sports Centre. Buzz through to the sports reception at the barrier to let them know you are there for korfball.

How?

If it's your first time, get in touch (via one of our social media channels or email) to let us know you're coming. All you'll need on the day is clothing you can move around in comfortably and a water bottle - we'll provide the rest!
